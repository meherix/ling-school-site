Privacy Policy for moduleBD

App Name: moduleBD — AI Co-Pilot with Student Intelligent Dashboard
Developer / Company: MEHErix
Last Updated: November 29, 2025

1. Introduction

moduleBD (“we”, “our”, or “us”) is an AI-powered educational app designed for students of Class 6–12. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, protect, and share personal information. By using moduleBD, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Children’s Privacy (Under 18)

  • moduleBD is intended for students aged 11–18.
  • Students under 18 must have parent/guardian permission to create an account.
  • We do not knowingly collect data from children without parental consent.
  • Parents may review, delete, or update their child’s information at any time.

If you believe a child used the app without permission, contact us at modulebd@meherix.com.

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Personal Information

  • Student name or nickname
  • Class level (e.g., Class 6–12)
  • Parent email or phone (optional but recommended)
  • Login credentials (email/phone or third-party login)

3.2 Educational Data

  • Subjects, chapters, and progress
  • Scores, quizzes, and performance analytics
  • AI tutor chat interactions
  • Learning history and recommendations

3.3 Device & Technical Data

  • Device ID (for security)
  • IP address
  • App version, crash reports
  • Log data (login time, failed attempts)

3.4 Uploaded Content

If the student uploads files (text, images, audio), they stay private unless explicitly shared. We do not collect precise GPS location, biometric data, advertising identifiers, or unnecessary sensitive data.

4. How We Use the Data

  • Create and manage accounts.
  • Personalize lessons and recommendations.
  • Provide AI tutoring and doubt-solving.
  • Track progress and performance.
  • Maintain app security and prevent fraud.
  • Improve educational features and user experience.
  • Communicate with parents where appropriate.

We never sell personal information.

5. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

  • Parental consent (for children under 18).
  • Legitimate educational purpose.
  • Performance of educational services.
  • Security and fraud prevention.

6. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell or rent data to advertisers or external companies. We may share data only with:

  • Service providers (cloud hosting, authentication, privacy-safe analytics).
  • Legal authorities if required by law, court order, or government request.

7. Data Storage & Security

  • AES-256 encryption for stored data.
  • TLS encryption for data in transit.
  • Access control for restricted data.
  • Regular security reviews and monitoring.

Data is stored in secure cloud servers compliant with relevant data protection standards.

8. Data Retention

  • Account data: kept until deletion request.
  • Learning data: retained for a limited period after inactivity (e.g., 18 months).
  • Uploaded files: deleted when the account is removed or upon request.
  • AI chat history: retained for a limited time for learning improvement and safety.

9. User Rights

Parents and students (where applicable) have the right to:

  • Access their data.
  • Update or correct information.
  • Delete data or request account deletion.
  • Download a copy of data (data portability).
  • Withdraw consent.

To exercise these rights, contact modulebd@meherix.com.

10. Cookies & Tracking

moduleBD does not use advertising cookies. We use only essential and performance cookies necessary for login, session management, and improving the app. See the Cookie Policy section below for details.

11. Parental Controls

Parents can:

  • Approve or deny account creation.
  • Review student activity where enabled.
  • Request full data deletion.
  • Manage notification and safety settings.

12. AI Safety & Moderation

Our AI follows strict youth safety guidelines, filters unsafe content, and is designed not to engage in harmful topics. It keeps chat content private and does not store sensitive personal history beyond what is required for learning support and safety.

13. International Data Transfers

If data is processed or stored outside Bangladesh, we ensure appropriate safeguards and compliance with applicable laws.

14. Links to External Websites

Some lessons may link to external educational websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices.

15. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The updated version will be posted in the app and on the website. Continued use means you accept the updated policy.

16. Contact Us

For privacy or data protection questions, contact:
Email: modulebd@meherix.com
Website: www.meherix.com/moduleBD
